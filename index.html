OET PREPARATION

The Occupational English Test assesses language skills specifically for healthcare and is accepted in Australia, Canada, Dubai, Ireland, Namibia, New Zealand, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, UK, Ukraine, and the US. Success in OET demonstrates that you have the right language skills to communicate effectively, safely, and accurately with patients and colleagues. Prepare with OET-endorsed tutors and standardized OET practice tests and materials to ensure the right guidance for success.

MEDICAL ENGLISH

Improve your basic and intermediate professional English with structured language specifically for doctors, nurses, therapists, and other healthcare professionals. Includes language for anatomy, patient diagnosis and care, conference subjects, and even everyday small talk. Essential vocabulary, grammar, speaking fluency techniques, and listening and reading skills.

Planning for OET? Course modules introduce OET-style exercises to already get you on track. Optional private or group classes available, taught by native-English expert tutors who help you practice true-to-life scenarios that you can immediately use on the job.
